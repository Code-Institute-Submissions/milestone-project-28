Physiotherapy

Welcome to the next generation of physiotherapy clinics. Throughout our experience as Chartered Physiotherapists we have had the pleasure of managing a wide variety of conditions including sports and exercise injuries, back and neck pain, joint replacements and everything in between. In Pain or Discomfort? We got you covered. Using a patient-centred approach with evidenced based practices, you will be provided with the tools necessary for your recovery. By educating you on the principles of pain and exercise, we help you to understand your problem, how it can be fixed and how to reduce the likelihood of it from reoccurring.

Personal Training

With thousands of hours dedicated to learning, practising and helping people with their rehabilitation and fitness goals, I have accumulated a vast and unique set of experiences between my time in public healthcare, high level sports and in the fitness industry; to provide you with one of the best personal training experiences out there. Whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle, get stronger or just feel healthier; you will provided with the principles necessary to achieve these, which you will have for the rest of your life. My goal is to guide you through a world that is full of mis-information and conflicting opinions by ego-bashing health and fitness professionals to get you to your goals; whether that is in the form of “rehabilitation” or “personal training”; in my mind, rehabilitation IS training, just in the presence of an injury.
